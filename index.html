Watch Creativity Unfold in Just 30 Minutes!

Welcome!

Welcome to the 30 Minute Film Festival. This is a fun project where people make short films in only 30 minutes from start to finish. That means you have to come up with a story, film it, and edit it super fast. It’s kind of crazy, but it makes everyone really creative and quick-thinking.

Right now, we are showing Late To Class, made by MMM Production. Three students made this film in just half an hour. It’s funny, a little mysterious, and full of energy. Watching it is kind of like watching a story unfold really fast, and you can see all the cool ideas they came up with in a short time.

Location: NYUAD Arts Center (C3)

Check out the film, read about the festival, or contact us if you have any questions. It's all about enjoying the films and celebrating what we created together.
